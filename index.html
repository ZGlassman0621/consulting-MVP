FAQ

Common questions.

What size winery is this for?
Any winery, any size. Our engagements are scoped around your three biggest priorities — the difference is what you need, not how big your production is.
What do I need to prepare before you arrive?
Very little. After booking, we'll send a short intake form asking about your current programs and top priorities. That's it — we come prepared so you don't have to do homework.
How does the 1-hour evaluation work?
One focused hour — on-site or remote — reviewing your current setup and identifying your biggest priorities. You'll leave with actionable insight. The $400 applies in full if you move forward.
What's included in the check-in calls?
Six 30-minute calls over six months, included with the Software Essentials engagement. We review how the playbook is performing, track changes, and adjust recommendations as your operation evolves.
Where do you work?
We serve wineries across North America. Travel logistics and any pricing adjustments are discussed as part of the booking process.
What are your payment terms?
Full payment due at playbook delivery. Payment plans available: 50% day of, 50% at delivery — or 25% at signing, 25% day of, 25% at delivery, 25% at your 2-month check-in. We accept cash, check, Venmo, and Zelle.
