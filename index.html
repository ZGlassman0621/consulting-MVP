FAQ

Common questions.

What size winery is this for?
Any winery, any size. Essentials is built for operations that want a focused, efficient audit of their core systems. Premium is for anyone who wants a deeper, more comprehensive dive — the difference is the depth of engagement, not the size of your production.
What do I need to prepare before you arrive?
Very little. After booking, we'll send a short intake form asking about your current programs and top priorities. That's it — we come prepared so you don't have to do homework.
How does the 1-hour evaluation work?
One focused hour — on-site or remote — reviewing your current software setup and identifying your biggest area of overspend. You'll leave with a concrete dollar figure. The $400 applies in full toward an Essentials or Premium engagement if you move forward.
How does the 6-month follow-up work?
Included with every engagement. Six 30-minute calls, one per month, where we review how the playbook is performing, track what's changed, and adjust recommendations as your operation evolves.
Where do you work?
We serve wineries across North America. Travel logistics and any pricing adjustments are discussed as part of the booking process.
What are your payment terms?
Full payment due at playbook delivery. Payment plans available: 50% day of, 50% at delivery — or 25% at signing, 25% day of, 25% at delivery, 25% at your 2-month check-in. We accept cash, check, Venmo, and Zelle.
